Project and Challenge Requirements

Focus

Projects that are focused on addressing a component of how to hack our civic space, the city of San Francisco.

Skillsets To Expect

The variety of skills/backgrounds of attendees will include:

    Developers

    Project Managers

    Community Organizers

    Designers

    Researchers

    Marketers

    Goverment Staff

    Urbanities

    Active Citizens

    Project Lead

    Each project requires a project lead to see the project from start to finish, attending the 3-day event. It is okay for two people to collaboratively lead the team.

    Goals and Structure

    The projects/proposals for the weekend should have:

    • Tangible goals that can be met by a team of 4-10 people over 2 full days of work
    • Projects that use varied skills and expertise levels
    • Clearly articulated challenges and tangible goals – think ‘project in a box’ that is ready for execution
    • Scalable goals to fit a team size between 4 and 10 people

    Starting Status

    Projects can be at different phases (ideation, testing a specific component, a few steps before launch, etc.) With that, structure the weekend’s project proposal so that new individuals can contribute to a specific goal or milestone.

    Timeline

    General info for the flow of the weekend:

    • Friday

      Friday Night: Project leads recruit members to their challenge at the Opening Party.

    • Saturday

      All Day: Work on Projects

    • Sunday

      All Day: Work on Projects

      Afternoon: Each project presents their work to the group
