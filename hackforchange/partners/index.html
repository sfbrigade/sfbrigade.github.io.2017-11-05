Partner on NDoCH SF

San Francisco has historically been known to welcome anyone and everyone into its Golden Gate. Yet that reputation is fading as The City becomes less affordable for some while a playground for others.

The time is ripe for an event to inspire the various communities of San Francisco around shared action to “hack” and re-connect the civic fabric of The City.

When the Mayor introduced the Shared Prosperity Agenda, we wanted to know how we, as engaged citizens of San Francisco, could help make these goals a reality. SF’s National Day of Civic Hacking is a chance for residents to prototype and test out their own solutions in these same issue areas. Afterward the event, projects created will be incubated within the Code for San Francisco weekly hack night during our Summer of Civic Tech.

We want to partner with you to make a better San Francisco, using the National Day of Civic Hacking. Below are different ways to support the event, starting a conversation together as partners.
