Sponsor National Day of Civic Hacking (NDoCH) SF

San Francisco is the global hub for technological innovation. Yet there exists serious tension between those who feel the benefits of that innovation and those who do not. The time is ripe for an event to inspire the various communities of San Francisco around shared action to “hack” the civic fabric of The City.

When the Mayor introduced the Shared Prosperity Agenda, we wanted to know how we, as engaged citizens of San Francisco, could help make these goals a reality. SF’s National Day of Civic Hacking is a chance for residents to prototype and test out their own solutions in these same issue areas. 300+ people will come to tackle challenges and projects around open data, housing, health, education, transportation, women’s empowerment, the environment, and anti-poverty.

We are proud to offer this opportunity to discover, network, and hack San Francisco. We hope you will help us reach our goals by supporting this global movement on a local level.
